History

Our rafting company began over a decade ago with a small group of river lovers and a few rafts. We focused on safety, adventure, and respect for nature, quickly growing into a trusted name for unforgettable whitewater trips.

Over time, we expanded our equipment and team while staying true to our mission. Today, we welcome adventurers from around the world, offering thrilling, safe, and memorable rafting experiences that connect people to the beauty of our rivers.
