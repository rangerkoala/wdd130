Our Trips

Los Dientes del Diablo

This adrenaline-packed trip takes you through the famous “Teeth of the Devil” rapids, where towering waves and sharp turns keep your heart racing. Perfect for thrill-seekers, this challenging route offers dramatic scenery, powerful currents, and an unforgettable whitewater experience guided by our expert team.

Big Mallard

Experience one of the region’s most iconic rapids on the Big Mallard run. Known for its massive waves and exhilarating drops, this trip delivers both excitement and breathtaking river views. Our skilled guides ensure you navigate safely while soaking in the beauty and raw power of nature.

Colorado River

Journey down the legendary Colorado River, a perfect blend of thrilling rapids and serene stretches. Along the way, marvel at towering canyon walls, unique rock formations, and crystal-clear skies. This adventure is ideal for those wanting both excitement and moments to relax and take in the scenery.
